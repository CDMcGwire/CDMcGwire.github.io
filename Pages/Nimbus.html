A lightweight RPG for tight schedules

What is this?

This is an experimental, pen-and-paper Roleplaying Game System. It is designed to be lightweight and quick, requiring little fore-knowledge on the playerâ€™s part, allowing for sessions to start sooner and require less preparation time.

Player Handbook

State of Development

I am currently running sessions of the game with a limited group to test the initial usability of the game. Currently trying to hone in on what makes the game unique from other RPGs.

What's working?

So far, the magic system is working as intended, allowing significantly more flexibility than, say, D&D's system while maintaining comparble or even less complexity. But it needs stress testing.

What needs improvement?

Moment to moment gameplay still doesn't feel distinguished enough from similar systems.

What's coming next?

Tweaks to conventional combat system that will hopefully allow more creativity on the players' part, while giving the DM tools to quickly and systematically determine results. Also a more defined approach to using non-combat skills in game, will possibly take the form of a "Role" system.
