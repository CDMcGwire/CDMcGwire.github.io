Adventuring Aid

This is a series of pages (generally for the GM) with extra rules to help with the less fundamental aspects of an adventure. Things like how to handle time and travel which, while usually important, can be house ruled with little effect on overall gameplay.

Bestiary

The bestiary is where you can find a set of proposed statistics for various monsters and enemies. GMs are, of course, free to invent as they will, but this should provide a decent sense of how combat is intended to be balanced statistically.

For players, this is where you'll find the statistics for any creature you are transformed into from various effects, like lycanthropy.
