Armor

Keeping you from death's door

As described in Armor & Defense, armor provides a passive defense, reducing how often physical attacks deal damage. There are two categories of armor: Simple and Heavy.

Simple armors are those that provide a modest amount of protection while remaining fairly natural to wear with minimal restriction of movement.

Heavy armors, as detailed in Combat Proficiencies, place rather large penalties to mobility, but make up for it with much greater defensive bonuses.

Simple Armors

  • Clothing and Robes: No defense bonus
  • Leather: +2 Defense
  • Chain: +3 Defense; -3 to Stealth; -1 to other Agility based proficiencies

Heavy Armors

  • Scale: +4 armor; Only suffers a base -3 to Agility
  • Half Plate: +5 Defense
  • Full Plate: +7 Defense; Cannot make Agility based proficiency rolls
