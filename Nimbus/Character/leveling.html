Leveling Up

Your power level is rising!

When you accomplish things, the GM will hand you experience points. When you collect enough experience points, you level up. When you level up, your skills get better.

Level Values

Here are the first few experience totals needed to level up, as well as the forumla for further levels. There is no hard cap on levels, but generally speaking any character above 16 is "High level." Experience does not reset at each level.

  • Level 1: 0 experience
  • Level 2: 1,000 experience
  • Level 3: 2,500 experience
  • Level 4: 6,250 experience
  • Level 5: 15,625 experience
  • Each proceeding level is 2.5x the total of the last

Bonuses per Level

Each level after first, your character gains 1 combat proficiency and 2 non-combat proficiencies.

At level 3 and every other level afterwards (level 5, 7, 9…) you may choose 1 new heroic trait.

At level 4, and every fourth level after (level 8, 12, 16…) you may increase one attribute by +1.
