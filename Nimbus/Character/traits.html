Traits

Selecting Traits

There are two main types of traits. Character Traits, which you pick when you first create the character. And Heroic Traits which your character gains as they adventure.

Character Traits

Selecting character traits involves picking a specific number of traits in each categories.

  • 1 Physique Trait
  • 1 Mental Trait
  • 1 Social Trait
  • 2 Profession Traits
  • 2 History Traits
  • 0-2 Extra Traits

Physique Traits

These define how your character is built physically. For instance, the Lean trait describes your character as being muscled, but light and agile. This wouldn't give as much brute power as the Brawny trait, but grants more mobility.

Mental Traits

These define how your character's mind functions.
