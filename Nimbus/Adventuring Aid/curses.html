Curses

Foul magic and blights

This section covers various permanent and semi permanent magical effects of the hexing variety. Things like lycanthropy, bad luck charms, possession, and the like.

If you picked the Cursed trait, this is where you will select your curse.

Transformative Curses
Lycanthropy

Demon Blood

You have the Blood of a Demon in your veins. Your body has twisted to take on some resemblance of the demon whose blood you share. The potential sources and their associated effects include:

  • Succubus - You share traits with a demon of lust. Your skin has a more pinkish tint, two small horns sprout from your head, and you exhibit an unnatural level of physical irresistability. All of your charm and deception rolls gain a +2. However, you cause a small amount of mental debilitation to people you charm. Causing them to be wary of you in the future.
